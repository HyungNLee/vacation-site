Explore the rustic countryside of Cartagena

Cartagena, a gorgeous fishing village on Colombia's Caribbean coast , has excellent beaches, a historic old town and beautiful colonial architecture. It's also one of the safest places in the country, so it's no wonder it's a popular port of call for cruise ships. Need a break from exploring the cobblestone streets? Stop at an outdoor cafe for excellent pastries and people-watching.

Start your adventure in the alluring village of Cartagena!

The vibrant capital of Bogotá

Bogotá is the capital and largest city in Colombia. A melting pot of people from around the country, it is diverse and multicultural, with a blend of modern and colonial architecture. The predominant colors in Bogotá are the green of the city’s many parks and the eastern mountains overlooking the sanctuaries of Monserrate and Guadalupe, and the rich red of its many brick buildings.

Bogotá is where cosmopolitan meets colonial!
